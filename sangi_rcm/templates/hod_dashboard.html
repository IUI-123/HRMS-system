    📚 Government Health Schemes

    All major schemes explained — eligibility, benefits, and how to apply

    Ayushman Bharat PM-JAY

    National Scheme

    World's largest government-funded health insurance scheme covering 50 crore+ citizens.

    • ✅ ₹5 lakh cover per family per year
    • ✅ 1,949+ medical procedures covered
    • ✅ 27,000+ empanelled hospitals
    • ✅ Cashless & paperless treatment
    • ✅ Pre-existing diseases covered from Day 1
    Visit pmjay.gov.in →

    Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana

    BPL Families

    Specifically targets families listed under SECC 2011 data for cashless hospitalization.

    • ✅ Targets 10.74 crore families
    • ✅ Covers secondary & tertiary care
    • ✅ No cap on family size
    • ✅ 3 days pre- & 15 days post-hospitalization
    Learn More →

    Janani Suraksha Yojana (JSY)

    Maternal Health

    Cash assistance to pregnant women from BPL families to encourage institutional delivery.

    • ✅ ₹1,400 in rural areas, ₹1,000 urban
    • ✅ Targets BPL, SC/ST women
    • ✅ Reduces maternal & infant mortality
    • ✅ Free delivery at government hospitals
    Learn More →

    Rashtriya Swasthya Bima Yojana

    Unorganised Workers

    Smart card-based health insurance for BPL workers in the unorganised sector.

    • ✅ ₹30,000 annual cover
    • ✅ Covers family of 5
    • ✅ Cashless hospitalization
    • ✅ Transport allowance ₹100/visit
    Learn More →

    Pradhan Mantri Surakshit Matritva

    Antenatal Care

    Free antenatal care for pregnant women on the 9th of every month at government health facilities.

    • ✅ Free check-up on 9th of every month
    • ✅ Blood tests, ultrasound included
    • ✅ High-risk pregnancy identification
    • ✅ Iron, folic acid supplements
    Learn More →

    Employees' State Insurance (ESI)

    Salaried Workers

    Medical and cash benefits for employees earning up to ₹21,000/month in organised sector.

    • ✅ Full medical care for employee & family
    • ✅ Sickness, maternity & disability benefits
    • ✅ Dependents' benefit on death
    • ✅ 149+ hospitals, 1,500+ dispensaries
    Learn More →
