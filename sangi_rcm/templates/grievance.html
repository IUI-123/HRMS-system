Grievance & POSH Policy
Your rights and the resolution process

The company is committed to a fair, respectful, and inclusive workplace.


📌 Scope: Applies to all permanent, contract, and probationary employees.


🔒 Confidentiality: Only designated HR will have access to your grievance.


🛡 Non-Retaliation: No employee will face adverse action for raising a genuine concern.


⏱ Response Time: HR acknowledges within 1 working day, resolves within 5 working days.


🚨 POSH: Sexual harassment complaints are handled by the ICC under the POSH Act, 2013.
